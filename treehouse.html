One or more of these images was taken from a (labeled for reuse) Google search highlighting tree houses in Costa Rica.

Guests at this imaginary house have an indoor restroom and hot tub, but may also wash up outside at the patio sink and/or bathe in the nearby* private waterfall, where the water is conveniently at a constant 37 degrees Celsius.

*Actual waterfall is in Tazmania, Australia.

Guests may relax in one of two living rooms, owing to the fact that this website designer couldn't choose between them. The smart tv has subscriptions to most tv streaming platforms, and includes a full cable package, because while communing with nature is great, cutting visitors off from television is heinous.
